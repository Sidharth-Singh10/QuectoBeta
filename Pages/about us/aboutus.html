About us

We are creating a special app that makes it way easier for you to order from the local shops around you. It's like a bridge that connects you with the local stores
Our mission

Individuals and families living in apartments or housing communities. Especially those who prefer the convenience of ordering from local shops but find the current process inconvenient Shop owners and businesses located in proximity to residential areas. Particularly those interested in expanding their customer base and improving their ordering and delivery processes. Tech-savvy individuals who are comfortable using mobile apps for their daily activities, including shopping. People who value and support local businesses and are interested in fostering community connections.

Shop with ease

We are bringing the revolution of online shopping with our new app Quecto. Unlike platforms like BlinkIt or Instamart that impose hefty delivery charges on every order, our app empowers each shop to set its own delivery fees based on local competition. Enhanced competition incentivizes shops to reduce delivery charges while ensuring swift deliveries, driven by customer reviews. In this ecosystem, shops strive to provide speedy service, creating a marketplace where businesses are motivated to deliver quickly and efficiently.
